Contact Options

night-bird Theme offers several options for Contact and allows you to insert various form codes, mail type, and much more. See the below steps that how to enable the contact options.

  • Navigate to Contact Options in your Wordpres admin sidebar.
  • Click on the Contact Tab option to customize the contact.
  • Contact-form1 - Click the form to show on contact-form details.
  • Contact-form2 - Click the form to show on contact-form details.
  • Contact-form3 - Click the form to show on contact-form details.
